Personal Project

This is one of the most impactful coding adventure I went on. I started with virtually no knowledge about pygame and ended up with a fantastic game!

Quick Explanation:

My final product was a game combining elements of a platformer and a shooter game. This game has 6 levels, dialogues that sum up to over one thousand words, UI, and most of the components you expect from a game. Since it is inspired by platformers such as Mario and shooter games, it includes mechanics such as shooting, healing, collecting coins and a timer system to stop you from wandering around the level. A permanent death system occurs when you lose all your health, but it still allows you to keep all your coins. If you fall into the void in a level, you return to the level selecting the menu at the cost of 10 coins.
